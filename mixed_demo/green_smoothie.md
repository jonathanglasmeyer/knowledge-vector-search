# Ultimate Green Smoothie

Packed with nutrients and surprisingly delicious! This green smoothie is the perfect way to start your day with energy.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 1 ripe banana (frozen works great)
- 1/2 avocado for creaminess
- 1 cup coconut water or almond milk
- 1 green apple, cored
- 1 inch fresh ginger
- Juice of 1/2 lime
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tsp spirulina (optional)
- Handful of ice cubes

## Instructions
1. Add liquid to blender first
2. Add leafy greens and blend until smooth
3. Add fruits, ginger, and other ingredients
4. Blend on high until completely smooth
5. Taste and adjust - add more lime for tang, banana for sweetness
6. Pour and enjoy immediately for best nutrition

## Health Benefits
- High in iron, vitamin K, and folate from spinach
- Healthy fats from avocado
- Fiber and potassium from banana
- Anti-inflammatory properties from ginger

Perfect for detox days or when you need a quick nutrient boost!
