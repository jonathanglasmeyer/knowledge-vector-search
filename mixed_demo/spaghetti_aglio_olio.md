# Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A classic Italian pasta dish with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. Simple, elegant, and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

## Ingredients
- 400g spaghetti
- 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and black pepper
- Optional: Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente
2. Heat olive oil in large pan, add garlic and red pepper flakes
3. Cook until garlic is golden but not burnt
4. Add drained pasta to the pan with some pasta water
5. Toss everything together, add parsley and seasonings
6. Serve immediately with optional Parmesan

Perfect for busy evenings when you want something delicious but don't have much time.
