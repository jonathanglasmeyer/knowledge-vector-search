# Overnight Oats

The perfect make-ahead breakfast that's ready when you wake up. Creamy, nutritious, and infinitely customizable.

## Base Recipe
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
- 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

## Popular Variations
- **Berry Bliss**: Mixed berries, vanilla, almond milk
- **Chocolate Peanut Butter**: Cocoa powder, peanut butter, banana
- **Apple Cinnamon**: Diced apple, cinnamon, walnuts
- **Tropical**: Coconut flakes, mango, lime zest

## Instructions
1. Mix all base ingredients in a jar or container
2. Add your chosen toppings and flavors
3. Stir well, cover, and refrigerate overnight
4. In the morning, stir and add fresh toppings
5. Enjoy cold or warm it up slightly

Perfect for busy mornings - just grab and go! Great for meal prep too.
