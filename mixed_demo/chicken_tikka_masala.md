# Chicken Tikka Masala

Britain's national dish! Tender chicken in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce with aromatic spices.

## For the Chicken
- 500g chicken breast, cubed
- 150g Greek yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated

## For the Sauce
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 200ml heavy cream
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- Salt to taste
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for at least 30 minutes
2. Cook chicken pieces until golden and set aside
3. Sauté onions until soft, add garlic and ginger
4. Add spices and cook until fragrant
5. Add tomatoes, simmer until thick
6. Stir in cream and cooked chicken
7. Simmer for 10 minutes, garnish with cilantro

Serve with basmati rice and naan bread for the full experience.
