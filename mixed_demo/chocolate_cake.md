# Decadent Chocolate Cake

Rich, moist, and intensely chocolatey - this is the ultimate chocolate cake for birthdays and celebrations.

## For the Cake
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup cocoa powder
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 cup strong black coffee
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

## For the Frosting
- 200g dark chocolate, melted
- 200g butter, softened
- 400g powdered sugar
- 1/4 cup cocoa powder
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 3-4 tbsp heavy cream

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C, grease two 9-inch cake pans
2. Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl
3. Whisk together wet ingredients including coffee
4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed
5. Divide between pans, bake for 30-35 minutes
6. Cool completely before frosting
7. Beat butter, add melted chocolate, then powdered ingredients
8. Add cream until desired consistency
9. Layer and frost the cake

The coffee enhances the chocolate flavor without making it taste like coffee. Perfect for celebrations!
